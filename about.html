About AccessDenied

Welcome to AccessDenied, a creepy Matrix-style raining code animation with hacker-themed messages designed for trolling and visual effect fun. This project combines nostalgic hacker vibes with playful trolling to mess with friends or just enjoy a cool terminal-style display on your browser.

Feel free to change colors, explore the messages, and have some lighthearted hacker fun. Created for the love of trolling and digital aesthetics!
